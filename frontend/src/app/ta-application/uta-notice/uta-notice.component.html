Thank you for your interest in joining a Computer Science course's teaching team as a Learning Assistant (LA). Our goal is to make the instructional support around computer science courses the best we humanly can. A significant factor toward achieving that goal is the positive energy, empathy, helpfulness, and passion LAs bring to serving *every* student enrolled.

This is a paid *job*, not a fellowship, scholarship, or volunteer role. If the exact dollar amount above minimum wage will make or break your interest in this role, it's probably not for you. Computer Science LAs are typically hired at a rate of $2,000 per semester for an expected 10-12 hour commitment per week. New COMP110 LAs are hired at a rate of $1,000 per semester for an expected 5-6 hour commitment per week.

To be eligible to apply as an LA you must have completed the course(s) you are applying for, with a B+ or better, by the end of the Fall semester. If you believe you are on track to complete a course this Fall with a B+ you are welcomed to apply for it.
