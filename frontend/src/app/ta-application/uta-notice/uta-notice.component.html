Computer Science UTA Application - Fall 2024

This form is for all students, including those who were Undergraduate Teaching Assistants (UTAs) in the Spring of 2024.

Application Deadline: May 10th at 11:59pm
Notification of Selection by August 20th

Thank you for your interest in joining a Computer Science course's teaching team as a Undergraduate Teaching Assistant (UTA). Our goal is to make the instructional support around computer science courses the best we humanly can. A significant factor toward achieving that goal is the positive energy, empathy, helpfulness, and passion UTAs bring to serving every student enrolled.

This is a paid job, not a fellowship, scholarship, or volunteer role. If the exact dollar amount above minimum wage will make or break your interest in this role, it's probably not for you. Computer Science UTAs are typically hired at a rate of $2,000 per semester for an expected 10-12 hour commitment per week. New COMP110 UTAs are hired at a rate of $1,000 per semester for an expected 5-6 hour commitment per week.

To be eligible to apply as a UTA you must have completed the course(s) you are applying for, with a B+ or better, by the end of the Spring semester. If you believe you are on track to complete a course this Spring with a B+ you are welcomed to apply for it.
